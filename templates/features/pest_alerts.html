INTEGRATED PEST MANAGEMENT

Our Process for Safe & Effective Results

1. Biological Control

Using natural enemies like predatory insects, parasitoids, and pathogens to control pest populations without relying heavily on chemical pesticides.

2. Cultural Practices

Modifying growing practices, such as altering planting dates or implementing crop rotation, to reduce pest establishment and survival.

3. Resistant Varieties

Deploying seed varieties naturally resistant or highly tolerant to specific local pests to secure baseline yields against infestations.
